FRUIT

Fruits are the sweet, fleshy, edible parts of plants that usually contain seeds. They develop from the flower of a plant and are a key part of the human diet because they are rich in vitamins, minerals, fiber, and natural sugars.

Types of fruits

  1. Citrus Fruit
    • Example: Orange, Lemon, Lime, Grapefruit
    • Rich in Vitamin C

  2. Stone Fruits
    • Example: Mango, Peach, Cherry
    • Have a large seed or "stone" inside

  3. Tropical Fruits
    • Example: Banana, Pineapple, Papaya
    • Thrive in warm climates

  4. Pome Fruits
    • Example: Apple, Pear
    • Have a core of seeds in the center

  5. Melons
    • Example: Watermelon, Cantaloupe
    • High in water content
